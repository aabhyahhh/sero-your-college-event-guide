Youth Climate Conclave 5th Edition

Organised by: TERI

Greetings from TERI!

TERI is a not-for-profit research organisation working on issues related to energy, environment, climate change, and sustainable development. The Education for Youth Empowerment (EYE) Area at TERI works with school students and youth to promote Education for Sustainable Development (ESD) in India and abroad.

To foster a community of young innovators dedicated to creating solutions that mitigate effects of climate change, the 5th edition of the 'Youth Climate Conclave' (YCC) is being convened. The conclave features a series of pre-event activities in the age category of 10–18 years and 19–25 years, including a photography competition, a two-day knowledge enrichment session (aligned with TERI's World Sustainable Development Summit 2025), and an exclusive excursion to provide evidence-based learning to empower youth to combat climate change.

We invite children and youth in the age category of 10-18 years and 19-25 years to participate in a Photography Competition. Attractive awards and incentives are in store for top performers.
